# Stakeholders

- **Sports Analytics Lead** — owns methodology and offline evaluation.
- **Product Manager** — defines “success metrics” and business use-case alignment.
- **Data Engineering** — data sourcing, validation, lineage, and refresh schedules.
- **ML Engineering** — training pipelines, model packaging, CI, and deployment patterns.
- **Risk/Compliance** — ensures proper disclaimers (not betting advice), monitoring, and auditability.
