# Stakeholders

## Primary stakeholders
- Credit Policy / Risk Management
- Underwriting Operations
- Compliance / Legal (fair lending)
- Data Science / ML Engineering
- Model Risk Management (MRM)

## Key review checkpoints
- Threshold and cost posture approval (false approvals minimized)
- Fairness and disparate impact review
- Monitoring dashboard review (monthly/quarterly)
- Incident response plan for drift or spikes in false approvals
